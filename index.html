are you interested in neuromorphic processing?

AI using neural networks replicates nature’s way to process information. This is fundamentally different from conventional, sequential information processing.

On the other hand, a new kind of microchips replicates nature’s way of processing information on the hardware level resulting in outstanding performance and energy efficiency.

For the first time these neuromorphic chips are now applied in practice. This allows next generation sensing and situation awareness capabilities of sensors and systems in medical, industrial and scientific applications.
